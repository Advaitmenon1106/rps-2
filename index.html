Rules of the game:-

  • Rock beats scissor, paper beats rock, scissor beats paper. ie., you choose the one that beats the other and it's a point against the computer for you
  • You, therefore, have 3 picks: 'Rock', 'Paper' and 'Scissor'.
  • You have a total of 5 games. Win as many as you can based on the above rules!
  • Note: This is a game of chance.
